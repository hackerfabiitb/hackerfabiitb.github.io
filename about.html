Introduction & Vision

Semiconductor chips power modern tech. Yet students rarely access fabrication tools. HackerFab IITB aims to change that by building a student-run, low-cost nanofabrication lab focused on hands-on learning and open-source tools.

Goals

  • Build India’s first student-built and run nanofabrication facility.
  • Create a bottom-up semiconductor ecosystem that complements national efforts.
  • Make fabrication affordable and aspirational for students.
  • Document and publish designs for easy replication.
