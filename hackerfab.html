Who We Are

We are a team of 7 students and growing, building a fast, low-cost semiconductor fab. Our goal is to make fabrication tools accessible for training and prototyping, helping students gain real hands-on experience. Inspired by CMU and Waterloo Hackerfabs.

Our Aims

  • Complete toolset to fabricate 2 µm pitch transistors by 2027.
  • Build deposition, sputtering, etching & furnace systems.
  • Fab-in-Box: cleanroom-free fabrication for colleges.
  • Develop EDA software for our in-house process flow.
  • Train 100+ students in two years and assist academic courses.
  • Contribute to the Indian Semiconductor Mission.

Our Philosophy

Our guiding philosophy is to democratize access to semiconductor fabrication. We believe that with open-source tools and a collaborative environment, students can learn by doing, innovate faster, and contribute directly to India’s semiconductor future.
