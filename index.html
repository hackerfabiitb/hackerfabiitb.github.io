01 · About

We build the machines
that build the chips.

Team at SemiX 2025 · IIT Bombay

"Making hands-on chip fabrication accessible across India."

Founded in August 2025, HackerFab IITB is India's first student-built microfabrication lab. We're part of the global HackerFab network — a movement to democratize semiconductor fabrication.

We design, build, and document every tool from scratch — open-source, student-run, and aimed at making the semiconductor industry accessible to students across India.
