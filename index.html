Apply to Join HackerFab IITB

×

Note: Only IITB students can apply. If you are at another college, join our Discord server instead. Send an email with the following:

Email:  hackerfabiitb@gmail.com
Subject:  Recruitment - Your Name
Length:  No longer than 1000 words

What to Include

1

Tell us about yourself

Who you are, your story, what you're curious and passionate about. Include your branch, programme and year of study.

2

Why do you want to join HackerFab IITB?

3

Tell us about your goals

Long-term and short-term. It's alright if they aren't well-formed or definitive.

4

What is your favourite part of the fabrication process and why?

5

Tell us about your technical skills

6

Tell us about something you've built

Preferably hardware. Provide links to any work you mention.

7

Tell us about something you dream about building

What is it? Why does it matter? How would you go about it?

8

Anything else you want us to know
