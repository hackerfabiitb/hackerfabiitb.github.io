How we began

Creating change from student up

Founded in August 2025, we're a group of students determined to make hands-on microfabrication education more accessible and engaging for everyone. Armed with our determination to spread this culture and graced by the support of our mentors and institute, we're building open-source, student-designed fabrication tools that anyone can use.
