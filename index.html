About

01 / 05
SemiX Conference · Sep 2025 · Mumbai

"Making hands-on chip fabrication accessible across India."

Founded in August 2025, we're a group of students at IIT Bombay determined to democratize semiconductor technology. We are part of the HackerFab global network — building real fabrication tools from scratch, sharing everything openly, aiming to become a stepping stone to India's semiconductor industry.
